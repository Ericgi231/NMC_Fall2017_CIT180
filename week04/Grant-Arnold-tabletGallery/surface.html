Surface Pro


As anticipation builds around Microsoft's forthcoming Surface Pro 5, the hybrid laptop-tablet space grows ever more crowded. For more than a year, the topnotch Surface Pro 4, first introduced back in October 2015, was the unchallenged standard-bearer in the PC tablet category.


Lately, however, Microsoft finds itself under increasing pressure from Samsung, Google, Lenovo, and Acer in the form of newer tablets featuring sleeker designs, lower price points, and an essential accessory -- i.e. keyboard -- that comes included. (Microsoft sells the Surface Pro 4's keyboard attachment separately, for $130.)
